About Event

Description

Participants will get to learn competitive programming which is required for placement in companies selection test and interview.Participants will be able to learn how to apply problem solving in an effective manner with optimum utilization of time and computational resources.

Rules

  1. All the first to final year students are allowed to participate
  2. Maximum members per Team can be 2.
  3. Use of Internet is not allowed.
  4. Problem resolution must be given in a specified time for a specific problem.
  5. Any programming language can be used.
  6. All participants should have valid ID cards of their respective institute, along with the registration receipt.
  7. All participants must be punctual. In case of failure, the participants will be eliminated.
  8. The decision of judges and the organizers will be final and binding to all.
  9. In case any assistance is needed during the event, the teams should approach only the event
  10. Participants are requested to check the PURSUITâ€™20 website, for any updation in rules before
  11. The decision of judges will be final and no arguments will be entertained
  12. Physical damage or irresponsible handling to any facility provided to participants will lead to disqualification.
  13. No person can be a part of multiple teams. In case of any clashes, the organizers of the event will not be responsible.

Event Structure

  1. The event will consist of two rounds.
  2. In each round programming question will be given to the team members
  3. Each team members output will help the other members of the same team
  4. There will be life lines like in KBC to help them score points
